Andrew Lee Farquhar

Composer who specialises in cinematic music

Andrew is an English film & tv music composer and songwriter. Andrew who is based in Northern England is carving a niche for himself as one of the foremost upcoming film, TV composers in the UK. He is an accomplished and creative composer specialising in cinematic works. He has provided music for worldwide corporate advertisements campaigns for Puig and other large multi-national companies. His music has featured on trailers for major films and hit drama television series in USA. With credits on BBC1, MTV, CBS and BBC2 documentaries, he has scored, sound-tracked or mixed animated short films, some of which have won awards. He has also been extensively involved in writing high end audio and highly useable music for large Production Music Libraries. He has an artistic flair and is a highly prolific pianist, which enables him to realise his compositional ideas as a music producer and composer. Most recently he continues to master the licensing and placing of his catalogue of Works in film, TV, Radio and most online mediums. He is currently pressing ahead with new compositional Works and has irons in the fire with a number of projects currently work in progress as he continues to grow his online presence via social media.
