Andrew Lee Farquhar

Composer who specialises in cinematic music

Andrew Lee Farquhar is a Music Composer & Producer from the UK and is currently making his mark in the Media Composing arena. Andrew has many years of experience in the music industry and has been recently extensively involved in writing high end audio / highly useable music for use in TV & Film. He is a proficient and highly musically creative Music Producer / Composer and continues to grow his unique catalogue of Works for use in film, TV, Radio and online mediums. He is an accomplished and creative composer specialising in a variety of genres including neoclassical piano or orchestral cinematic music but can work in other genres too. His music has been played all over the world to large audiences. He is currently working on a number of projects as he continues to grow his online presence via social media.
